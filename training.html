Introduction to Drupal: Mentored Training with Build a Module and Promet Source

Presented by: craychee, dobrzyns, generalredneck, marcdelay

The Build a Module curriculum allows you to focus on exactly what you need to know. Our mentors, all of whom are Drupal professionals, will help you work through a portion of the videos in the classroom. Plus, when 3 months of free access to the Build a Module video library so you can continue to pursue your goals long after the training is over.
